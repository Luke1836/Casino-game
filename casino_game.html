Casino Royale

Rules and Regulations

  1. Each players will get a pair of cards.
  2. Th player has to first click the Let's play button or the 'Generate Numbers' button. Then click on the 'Roll it!' button.
  3. If the sum of the cards exceeds the lucky number, the person loses.
  4. If it is less than the lucky number, the person gets another try.
  5. The winner is decided unanimously, if the lucky number is drawn.
  6. Otherwise the person with the highest sum wins.
  7. The 'Genreate Numbers' button generates numbers randomly for the player. Don't use it too much.
  8. This is a gam made for fun it is absolutely free and not for gambling purposes.

What is a lucky number?

It is a number which determines the winner of the game, and is decide by the host of the game.
